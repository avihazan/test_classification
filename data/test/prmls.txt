PURCHASE PRICE AND ALLOCATION  
  
2.1 

Purchase Price payable by the Purchaser to Vendor for Vendor Assets is as follows:  
  
  


* * *

  
(a)

(i) 

US$37,500 on the Effective Closing Date, (the "Initial Payment") which will purchase 250,000 shares of Lexaria and 250,000 warrants to purchase an additional 250,000 shares from the Lexaria treasury at a price of US$0.20 each payable directly to Lexaria Corp, prior to May 31, 2012, and
