ARTICLES OF AMENDMENT 

TO 

ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION 

OF 

MACKIE DESIGNS INC. 

Pursuant to RCW 23B.10.020, the undersigned corporation adopts the following Articles of Amendment to its Articles of Incorporation: 

FIRST: The name of the corporation is Mackie Designs Inc. 

SECOND: The Articles of Incorporation are hereby amended as follows: 

ARTICLE I 

Name

The name of this Corporation is LOUD Technologies Inc. 

THIRD: The amendment does not provide for an exchange, reclassification, or cancellation of issued shares. 

FOURTH: In accordance with the provisions of RCW 23B.10.020, the amendment was approved and adopted by the Board of Directors on August 6, 2003. Shareholder action was not required. 

FIFTH: The amendment shall be effective September 15, 2003. 

Dated this 29 day of August, 2003 

MACKIE DESIGNS, INC.
